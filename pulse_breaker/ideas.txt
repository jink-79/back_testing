--------------------------------------------------------------
  IDEAS FOR v2 — PARAMETER TWEAKS
--------------------------------------------------------------

[PENDING] Wider trailing stop offset
  Change: TRAIL_OFFSET_PCT 2% → 3%
  Reason: 75 of 131 exits via trailing stop but many cut winners
          too early (2–4 weeks held). Wider offset lets winners
          breathe and potentially increases avg win %.
  Expected impact: Lower trade count, higher avg win, lower win rate.
  File: weekly/scripts/v2/02_strategy_v2.py

[PENDING] RS slope filter
  Change: Add condition — Mansfield RS must be rising for 3+
          consecutive weeks (not just RS > 0 at breakout)
  Reason: TCS sits at 50% WR — many entries happen when RS
          is barely positive and flat/falling. A rising RS
          confirms genuine outperformance momentum.
  Expected impact: Fewer trades, higher quality entries, better WR.
  File: weekly/scripts/v2/ or v3/

[PENDING] Minimum breakout range check
  Change: Skip signals where breakout candle range
          (high - low) / low < 3%
  Reason: Tight, choppy breakouts from narrow bases tend to
          reverse quickly. Most 1–3 week structure break losers
          likely come from these.
  Expected impact: Fewer false breakouts, cleaner trade log.
  File: weekly/scripts/v2/ or v3/
